The Journey

From Agency to
Community Powerhouse

A decade of evolution, from marketing agency to India's most transformation-focused digital community.

2015
Started as a Marketing Agency
The seed was planted. Social Eagle began its journey as a digital marketing agency.
2016
Evolved into an Academy
Students who couldn't afford agency fees needed training. The academy was born from necessity.
2017 - 2019
International Expansion
Fortune 500 clients: Nestle, Nature Valley, Haagen-Dazs, Singapore Management University, Merrill Lynch. The world took notice.
2020
21 Day Challenge Launched
The online era began. What started as a challenge evolved into annual subscriptions and lifetime memberships.
2021 - 2024
Programs Multiplied
EOS, Digital Entrepreneurship, Agency Boardroom, EnergyX, Sales Operating System, Reclaim Your Life. Each program solving a specific need.
2025 - NOW
Social Eagle Community (SEC)
All programs unified under one roof. Evolved into a school model. AI community launched. The next chapter begins.
